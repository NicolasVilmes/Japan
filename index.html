JAPAN

Japan is an island country in the Pacific Ocean with a population of over 126 million people, making it the world’s tenth most populous country. The country is divided into 47 prefectures in eight regions, with Hokkaido being the northernmost prefecture and Okinawa being the southernmost. The capital of Japan is Tokyo, which is also the country’s largest city. The Greater Tokyo Area is the most populous metropolitan area in the world.
